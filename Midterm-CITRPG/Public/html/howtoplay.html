How To Play



a. Player Mode

- At the beginning of the game, you have the option to choose between 1 or 2 player mode. Select the mode that best suits your preference.

b. Choose Your Character

- Pick a character that resonates with you from the available options. Each character has its unique traits and abilities.

c. Select Your Major

- Decide on one of the three majors that you want to pursue. Your choice will influence the questions you'll face during the game.

- Brace yourself for an exciting and educational adventure! You will be presented with a series of 12 questions related to the major you selected.

- Each question will test your knowledge and skills in your chosen field. Be prepared for a diverse range of topics and challenges.

- Answer each question to the best of your ability. Your score will be based on the accuracy and speed of your responses.

- Strive for a perfect score! If you manage to answer all 12 questions correctly, you'll be rewarded with a special prize at the end of the game.

- Reach the end of the game with a flawless score to claim your victory. Celebrate your accomplishment and enjoy the satisfaction of mastering your chosen major.

- If you achieve a perfect score, you will be eligible to receive a prize. Follow the on-screen instructions to claim your reward and revel in your success.

- Most importantly, enjoy the journey! Whether you're playing alone or challenging a friend, the game is designed for both entertainment and education. Good luck, and may the best player win!
